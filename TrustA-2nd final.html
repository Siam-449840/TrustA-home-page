Secure Delivery Process

Our step-by-step process ensures secure, transparent, and reliable transactions for both buyers and sellers.

1

Order Confirmation

Buyer places order with payment to TrustA Escrow account. A unique TrustCode is generated and bound to the buyer's account for secure verification.

2

Professional Packaging

Product is professionally packaged with dual TrustCode labels (external QR code + internal numeric code) to prevent tampering and ensure authenticity.

3

Real-time Tracking

Buyer tracks delivery in real-time through the TrustA app. Delivery personnel can only contact buyers through the secure in-app system.

4

Product Verification

Buyer inspects product upon delivery, checking for damage, authenticity, and the internal TrustCode sticker to ensure the product hasn't been tampered with.

5

TrustCode Verification

Buyer scans the QR code to confirm delivery. Funds are automatically released to the seller and delivery personnel, completing the secure transaction.
